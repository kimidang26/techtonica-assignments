How to Make Sushi Bake

Sushi Bake is the latest gastronomic trend that gives you a deconstructed version of the original Japanese rolled Sushi. This delicious recipe also comes with my own method of making homemade Furikake.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups freshly cooked rice
  • 3 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup of Furikake
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 200 grams cream cheese
  • ¼ cup Japanese mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon Sriracha - adjust as desired
  • 20 sheets Korean roasted seaweed sheets - or use nori sheets

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, pour a mixture of rice vinegar, salt and sugar into the freshly cooked rice and toss until evenly distributed. Then set the bowl aside
  2. In another bowl, combine the cream cheese, Japanese mayonnaise, and Sriracha, then mix.
  3. To assemble your baked sushi, In a rectangle pan (8x6in), spread the sushi rice evenly then lightly press down. Sprinkle a thin layer of Fukikake on top of rice until fully covered.
  4. Bake at 200°C/390°F for 15 to 20 minutes.
  5. Take the baked sushi out of the oven and scoop a portion onto a plate and ENJOY!
